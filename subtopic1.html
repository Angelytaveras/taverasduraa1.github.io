TeleRehabilitation

URL Telerehabilitation
Site Author Science Direct
Abstract In this site, they introduce telerehabilitation and its uses. Telerehabilitation is a form of care that allows patients to receive treatments and check ups virtually. In the physical therapy field it has become very beneficial. It has become an essential part of the short and long term monitoring process for patients. A big conflict in this field is patients not finishing their sessions. Not only does this new technology increase the percentage of patients completing their treatments, it also aids therapists in keeping up with each patient one on one. Additionally, telerehabilitation provides accessibility to many new patients. It has become the new efficient and effective way to receive treatment.
URL Benefits Of Telerehabilitation
Site Author Bardavon
Abstract In this site, the benefits of telerehabilitation are discussed further. It was said that even before the COVID-19 pandemic telerehabilitation was being used and it was effective. Some of the benefits discussed on this site are enhanced patient accessibility and reach, reduced rates of missed visits and lastly, cost saving. While these are great improvements the site still acknowledges the few cons that come with telerehabilitation. Some of them being technology difficulties for patients, issues with internet connectivity and the lack of hand-on approach. When trying new things it's important that all the pros and cons are acknowledged. This site does just that while also including their recommendations.
